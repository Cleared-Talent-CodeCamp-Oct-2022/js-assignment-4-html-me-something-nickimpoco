Investment

One

Before we get into the game, I think the best place to start
with any new undertaking is with yourself. It's important to manage your expectations before learning something new.

Two

Do you want to have a good time playing with friends?
Do you want to climb the ladder online?
Do you want to travel and win tournaments?
Your answer to this question will determine how much time you should put into it. There is also no wrong answer, only expectations that don't match your time and effort invested.
